Northwest State Birds and Flowers

Hello, curious reader, and welcome to Northwest State Birds and Flowers. Here you will find information and facts about the official state bird and flower for each US State that make up the Northwest region. I've lived in this area for the last ten or eleven years and I wanted to show some appreciation for it.

On my site I will be covering the official flowers and birds of the five states in the Northwest region of the United States. These include Idaho, Montana, Oregon, Washington, and Wyoming.
